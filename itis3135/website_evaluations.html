Arngren

Arngren is a highly cluttered classified and gadget site filled with tiny images, dense text, and scattered elements. There is no clear layout or structure, making navigation difficult.

  • Contrast: Many colors are used with no hierarchy, and text often overlaps busy backgrounds, reducing readability.
  • Repetition: There is no consistent styling of fonts, colors, or elements, resulting in visual chaos.
  • Alignment: Elements appear randomly placed with no recognizable grid or alignment structure.
  • Proximity: Unrelated items are crammed together with little spacing, making it unclear what belongs together.
