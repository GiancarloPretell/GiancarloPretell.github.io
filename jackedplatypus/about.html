Jacked Platypus Protein Co.

Jacked Platypus Protein Co. is a small, whimsical snack company inspired by the most unexpectedly athletic creature: the platypus. Our mascot is a shredded, gym-loving platypus who motivates everything we create.

We specialize in high-protein shakes, goofy platypus-shaped energy bites, and limited-edition snacks made for students, athletes, and anyone who wants healthier options that still feel fun and creative.
